Chicken Tikka Masala

Home

Description

A rich and creamy Indian-inspired curry made with marinated chicken in a spiced tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • boneless chicken thighs
  • plain yogurt
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • garam masala
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • tomato sauce
  • heavy cream
  • butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the chicken in a pan until browned, then set aside.
  3. Melt butter in the pan and add tomato sauce and spices.
  4. Stir in cream and simmer until thickened.
  5. Add chicken back to the sauce and simmer until fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan.
