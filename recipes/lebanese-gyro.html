Lebanese Gyros

Home

Description

Flavorful spiced meat wrapped in warm pita with fresh vegetables and creamy garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • chicken or beef strips
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • lemon juice
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • cinnamon
  • pita bread
  • tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • garlic sauce (toum)

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat with olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and spices.
  2. Cook the meat in a hot pan until browned and cooked through.
  3. Warm the pita bread.
  4. Fill pita with meat, vegetables, and garlic sauce.
  5. Wrap and serve immediately.
